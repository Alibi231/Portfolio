Life Rocks!

March 2nd-6th, 2026

A first person stone skipper made for Sheridan's Winter 2026 Design Week. The design challenge was making a game around a unique verb, with my team choosing "skipping". The Game is a sandbox where the player times their throws to skip rocks at targets and go for a high score. I worked on both the games assets and C# programming in Unity.
